KAISER BEAR

KAISER BEAR is a multimedia production house that specializes in photo and motion shoots. Founded by Photographer / DP / Director Justin Schaefers in 2013, Kaiser Bear aims to take the fashion and commercial world to the next level. With years of experience working alongside some of the top names in the fashion and motion world, Justin has honed his skills in lighting and composition to bring forth some exciting new work. "Drop me in the jungle with a machete and camera and we are good to go."
